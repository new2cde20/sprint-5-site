    • I'd say to anyone interested in traveling to definitely do it! It's a once in a lifetime experience and definitely changes you and your perspective on life. I got involved with traveling through my school so if you're a student at any level, definitely check out any programs that your school may have. They often can help with financial aid if that's a concern. There are also multiple programs outside of academic settings, so if you're not a student, you can still travel! If you Google "traveling programs" you'll find a plethora of information online. There are also traveling fairs you can attend - I attended one in high school and found a lot of programs that way. Often, these programs, whether you find them online or at in-person events, offer financial assistance and payment plans if money is a concern. They often also have a list of information with answers to frequently asked questions and concerns.
